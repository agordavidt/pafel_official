Who We Are

Pafel Solutions Nig Ltd

Pafel Solutions Nigeria Limited is a trusted HVAC and built-environment leader based in Abuja, delivering superior comfort and air quality solutions across Nigeria.

With deep technical knowledge and engineering expertise, we specialize in the end-to-end supply, bespoke design, seamless installation, and proactive maintenance of premium climate control systems, including industry-leading Daikin Air Conditioners.

Our unwavering commitment to excellence, energy efficiency, and technical precision ensures optimal performance for our diverse residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Purpose, direction, and the standard we hold ourselves to.

Mission

To enhance indoor air quality and lifestyle comfort by delivering sustainable, energy-efficient, and reliable HVAC architectures engineered precisely to our clients' architectural demands.

Vision

To scale engineering excellence across West Africa, setting the benchmark for institutional climate control, sustainable built environments, and absolute professional dependability.
