Alpha Praxis Headquarters & Commercial Complex

The Context

As a multi-tenant commercial complex, the facility required a high-capacity climate solution capable of handling variable thermal loads across different office layouts without driving up energy overheads.

Engineering Solution

We engineered and executed the precision piping and installation of a Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV) ceiling cassette system. This setup allows localized temperature control for individual zones, ensuring optimized airflow mapping across the entire corporate infrastructure.

The Outcome

Delivered an energy-efficient, whisper-quiet cooling matrix that adapts dynamically to building occupancy, resulting in lower operational costs and maximum tenant comfort.

Executive Private Residence

The Context

The architectural brief for this premium estate demanded a powerful, multi-room cooling system that would completely preserve the sophisticated interior aesthetics and high ceilings of the home.

Engineering Solution

Supplied and installed premium split ceiling-concealed units. By housing the bulk of the mechanical hardware within the ceiling voids, we delivered seamless climate control accessible only via minimalist linear diffusers that blend directly into the drywall architecture.

The Outcome

Achieved a pristine, uncompromised interior design profile with multi-zone cooling control, offering personalized luxury comfort room by room.

Judges Quarters (Yellow House)

The Context

This high-profile institutional residence required a comprehensive, centralized cooling overhaul that balanced heavy-duty structural durability with quiet, dignified living spaces.

Engineering Solution

We deployed a comprehensive VRV Ceiling-Concealed Ducted System. This architecture utilizes hidden duct networks to distribute balanced, conditioned air uniformly across massive floor plates, managed by centralized smart thermostats.

The Outcome

Eliminated hotspots entirely across the property while maintaining an ultra-low acoustic footprint, proving that massive cooling power can be completely silent.

Bonzi Complex

The Context

Located in a high-traffic commercial zone, this venue experiences rapid spikes in foot traffic and high ambient heat-loads, demanding an aggressive, industrial-grade cooling response.

Engineering Solution

We supplied and commissioned heavy-duty, Large Ducted Central Systems. The layout was calculated to maximize volume air changes per hour, driving massive quantities of cooled air through engineered high-velocity duct networks designed for dense commercial spaces.

The Outcome

Rapid thermal recovery times—the system can cool down high-occupancy spaces within minutes of full activation, even during peak afternoon exterior temperatures.

The Oasis Auditorium

The Context

Large worship auditoriums present unique HVAC challenges: massive open vertical spaces, expansive ceiling heights, and sudden, extreme weekend occupancy loads.

Engineering Solution

We designed a hybrid cooling strategy, supplying and installing a high-capacity mix of Floor-Standing and Ceiling Cassette Units. The cassettes provide widespread down-draft cooling from above, while the standing units offer immediate, high-volume perimeter air displacement right at the crowd level.

The Outcome

Achieved perfect thermal equilibrium throughout the vast hall, ensuring a comfortable environment for worshippers from the front rows all the way to the back gallery.
