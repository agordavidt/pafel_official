Featured Case Studies

Three problems, and exactly how we solved them.

Data Center · 1200 Tons

Lagos Financial Tech Hub

Challenge

The client's legacy CRAC setup had a single point of failure — any unit going down during peak trading hours risked overheating racks serving live financial transactions.

Solution

We designed a redundant N+1 CRAC configuration with real-time temperature monitoring, sized from actual rack heat-load data rather than floor-area estimates.

Result

100% uptime since commissioning, with energy draw down roughly 18% against the previous setup.

Industrial · Cold Storage

Kano Cold Storage Facility

Challenge

Inconsistent temperatures from an aging compressor system were causing regular spoilage losses on perishable stock.

Solution

Retrofit with energy-efficient compressors and upgraded panel insulation, plus a maintenance schedule built around the facility's actual load cycles.

Result

Spoilage incidents dropped by roughly 90%, with the retrofit paying for itself in an estimated 14 months.

Commercial · Medical

Wuse Medical Diagnostics Centre

Challenge

Imaging equipment required tight temperature and humidity tolerances that the building's original split units couldn't hold consistently.

Solution

Installed a precision VRF system with dedicated humidity control zoning around the imaging suite, separate from general office cooling.

Result

Zero equipment downtime due to climate variance since installation.
