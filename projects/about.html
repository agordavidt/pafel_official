Our Story

Started by engineers tired of watching bad installs fail.

Pafel Solutions Nigeria Limited was founded after our lead engineers spent years fixing HVAC systems that were never designed correctly in the first place — undersized units, ductwork that fought the building instead of working with it, and maintenance plans nobody followed.

We built Pafel around a simpler premise: run the load calculations properly, install to spec, and show up before something breaks, not after. That discipline is what's let us grow from small residential jobs to mission-critical industrial cooling without cutting corners along the way.
