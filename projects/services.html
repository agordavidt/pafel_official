Service 01

Custom HVAC Design & Engineering

Over-specify a system and you waste capital. Under-specify it and you destroy equipment early. We run real thermal load calculations before a single pipe is laid — this is the service that determines whether everything after it works.

Built for architects, project engineers, and developers before ground is broken. We work alongside your MEP team and hand over documentation your contractors can actually use.

1
Site Survey & Load Calculation

Local weather data, solar heat gain, and building materials factored into an exact heat-extraction figure for your specific site — not a rounded estimate.

2
Ductwork & Airflow Modelling

Duct paths designed for even airflow and no cold spots, with static pressure checked before installation begins.

3
Equipment Selection

VRF, chillers, or split systems specified to hold up under Nigeria's grid fluctuations, not just rated capacity on paper.

4
Documentation & Handover

Full drawings and specification sheets delivered for contractor coordination — the same documents your facilities team will use in year five.
