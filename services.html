Service 03

Structural Refrigerant Conduit Piping

One of the most common HVAC failures in Nigeria: refrigerant leaks hidden behind finished walls. We lay flawless copper conduit piping built to withstand a lifetime of systemic pressure.

Leaking refrigerant lines cause compressor failure, structural damp, mould propagation, and complete system loss. Proper piping is non-negotiable — and must be tested before commissioning.

Type L thick-wall for commercial
Insulation
High-density elastomeric foam
Test Protocol

Service 05

Advanced Diagnostics & Troubleshooting

When conventional checks fail to identify performance losses, vibration, noise, or unexplained energy spikes — our diagnostic team deploys specialist instrumentation to find the root cause.

  • Vibration analysis — detects bearing wear and mechanical imbalance
  • Thermal imaging — reveals heat buildup and refrigerant flow anomalies
  • Refrigerant leak tracing — electronic and UV dye detection
  • Electrical log analysis — motor draw profiling and fault code history
  • Duct pressure testing — mapping dead zones and airflow losses
  • Legacy system assessment — compatibility and upgrade pathway reports
